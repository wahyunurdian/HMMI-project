We Believe In Our Dreams

Honda Precision Parts Manufacturing established in Indonesian as a contribution to the development of the world automobile industry and is a further breakthrough from Honda.

Established on an area of 130,000 square meters in the industrial area Indotaisei Cikampek, Karawang, West Java, Indonesia On June 6, 2002.

In September 2003 has started production of Automatic Transmission and Engine Valve which is supplied to the domestic and foreign markets and in 2006 it has begun production of a 4WD.

And recently in 2014 has started production of the M-CVT transmission Mission which is a new framework and conduct inhouse production for CVT Belt and Pulley.

By doing so, Honda Precision Parts Manufacturing become more competitive and gain consumer confidence Honda Precision Parts Manufacturing, through delivery of products to the world HPPM determined to be true as Power Train Leading Company.
